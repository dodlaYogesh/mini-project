Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" link on our homepage. Fill in the required information, including your name, email address, and a secure password. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the registration process.
2. Can I book gas without an account?
While browsing the available gas options is possible without an account, you need to create an account to complete the booking process. This ensures a secure and personalized experience for our users.
3. How can I track my gas delivery?
Once your gas order is confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking link. You can use this link to check the real-time status and estimated delivery time of your gas order.
